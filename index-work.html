At the moment I am honing my skills at
Eastern Washington University while
working towards a BFA in Visual
Communications and Design. I am
originally from Hoquiam, WA but have
lived in Spokane, WA for 12+ years. I
have a 16 month old daughter. Her and
my wife are by far the best things that
have happened in my life. I do not know
what I would do without them. I love
photography, golf and all things
creative. I make an attempt to play the
guitar and piano. The best quote I have
heard this year is, “Play is the engine
that powers education.”

I am currently working in the restaurant industry until I
finish school. I am always looking for freelance work so
shoot me an email if you are interested. If you are just
looking to chill out a bit and have a kick-ass martini
come by Twigs Bistro and Martini Bar and I’ll get you
set up! During the summer you can find me at any one
of the nearby golf courses!
